Soba Noodles

Description

This is a cold refreshing dish that will be perfect for the summer

Ingredients

  • One serving of Soba noodles
  • Tsuyumen
  • Dried Seaweed Snack
  • Plum Syrup
  • Two Eggs
  • Sesame Seeds
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the soba noodles and the two eggs for 7 mins
  2. Create the sauce by mixing 1 tbsp of sesame oil, 1 tbsp of plum syrup, 2 tbsp of soy sauce, and 2 tbsp of tsuyumen, and a dash of sesame seeds.
  3. Drain the noodles and eggs after cooking and put both into a cold ice bath.
  4. Peel the eggs.
  5. Put the noodles, eggs, and seaweed into bowl and add the sauce onto the ingredients.
  6. Enjoy!
